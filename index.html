Card Matching Pairs

Train your memory with this beautiful concentration style card game with playful sounds, fun graphics and smooth animations. 

Card Matching Pairs is an educational card game where you try to match pairs of cards. The game starts with all card face down. When you tap a card, it flips over. Tap another to try and find it's match. The game is over when you match all the cards.
